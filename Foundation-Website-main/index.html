What is this website about?

This website is an informative website developed by a team called TheBest. We provide information about the anime series "My Hero Academia".

~ Season 2 ~

The heroes are back!

The U.A. High School Class 1-A students learn that they’re all going to a group training camp in order to get stronger, even though their teacher Shoto Aizawa (a.k.a. Eraserhead) clarifies that not all of them passed their exams. (He also admits that all of the teachers took it somewhat easy on their students, which has the kids understandably upset.) But what better way to celebrate their upcoming training camp session than with a group trip to the mall?

My Hero Academia The Movie: The Two Heroes [Coming Soon]

My Hero Academia the Movie: The Two Heroes is an original story that delves into the mysterious background of the world’s greatest hero, All Might. Originally written by Horikoshi Kouhei, My Hero Academia was first serialized in July 2014 in Weekly Shonen Jump. In a world where most of the population have powers, or “Quirks", protagonist Midoriya Izuku has always dreamed of following in the footsteps of All Might, the best pro hero around. Despite lacking a Quirk, Izuku is one day given an unexpected chance to pursue his heroic nature when he meets the legend himself.
