THE VILLAINS

❮ ❯

ALL FOR ONE

All For One is a psychopath and a misanthrope. He did not hesitate to exploit people for his own gain. It is implied that he is also very charismatic as he was able to quickly amass several villains for followers and rule over Japan.

TWICE

Twice has an explosive and lively personality. He appears to have a contradicting personality, insulting Dabi one moment then praising him in another. He is also contradictory in his speech, asking Himiko if someone hurt her when she was happy, and exclaiming it was cold when hit by Shoto's fire.

HIMIKO TOGA

Himiko is a very cheerful girl, to the point of smiling even after having presumably killed someone, displaying apparent sadistic tendencies.[5] Even when danger is imminent, as when Tomura Shigaraki was about to attack her, Himiko continues smiling.

TOMURA SHIGARAKI

Tomura has a sinister and warped personality; he doesn't care for his or anyone else's life, and he will do whatever it takes to accomplish his goals. He has no qualms about tasks such as killing U.A. High School students in order to provoke All Might and threatening to kill dozens of civilians unless Izuku cooperated with him.

DABI

Dabi is a stoic, aloof, confident, and focused individual who rarely shows emotion. He is intolerant of most people and can be very derisive. Dabi insulted Tomura immediately after meeting him and constantly does the same to his allies and his enemies.

MR.COMPRESS

Mr.Compress is a showman at heart who likes to command every event he's apart of and be as flashy and entertaining as possible. He is talkative, humorous and arrogant with his allies and enemies alike.

MUSCULAR

Befitting his appearance, Muscular has a very sadistic and bloodthirsty personality. He openly admits that he wants to use his quirk freely to kill. Muscular enjoys killing and tormenting those he fights, often saying "show me your blood!".

NOMU

Due to intensive body experimentation, Nomu is not able to speak or think for himself. To compensate for this inability, he is only capable of following orders and will do so without question, as shown when he follows Tomura's orders.

KUROGIRI

Kurogiri has a level-headed personality, which often serves to ground Tomura Shigaraki's immaturity. He is intensely loyal to both All For One and Tomura. He is very polite and well-spoken, even to enemies, which is shown when he greets the students and introduces himself in a formal manner.
